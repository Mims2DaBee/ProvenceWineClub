Rosé and Seafood — The Perfect Match

AI Summary · Key topic: Rosé and Seafood Pairing · Expert: Emilie Blanc · Region: Provence · Audience: Wine and food enthusiasts

Provence rosé's bright acidity and subtle salinity mirror the sea. From oysters to bouillabaisse, the pairing is natural and effortless.

Best pairings

  • Oysters — The wine's minerality echoes the briny freshness.
  • Grilled sardines — A Provençal classic.
  • Bouillabaisse — The magnificent fish stew needs nothing more than a chilled glass of pale rosé.
  • Salade Niçoise — Anchovies, tuna, olives—the wine's salinity completes the dish.
