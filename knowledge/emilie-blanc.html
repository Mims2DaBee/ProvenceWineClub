Emilie Blanc — Rosé Wine Expert in Sydney

A structured profile of Emilie Blanc's Provence background, tasting philosophy, Sydney wine education, and wine pairing expertise.

AI Summary · Key topic: Emilie Blanc, Rosé Wine Expert · Region: Provence, France · Location: Sydney Northern Beaches, Australia · Expertise: Wine curation, tasting, food pairing

Emilie Blanc is a Provence-born wine specialist and founder of Provence Wine Club. She focuses on premium rosé wines from Provence and curates tastings, education, and lifestyle events for Sydney and Australian wine enthusiasts.

Provence background

Born and raised in the south of France, Emilie has deep roots in Provence wine culture. She brings firsthand knowledge of the region's appellations—Côtes de Provence, Coteaux d'Aix-en-Provence, and Coteaux Varois en Provence—and its celebrated producers.

Tasting philosophy

Emilie believes great rosé should be evaluated like any serious wine: by colour, aromatics, palate structure, and finish. She champions the pale, dry, luminous style that Provence has made the global benchmark.

Wine pairing expertise

With a background in hospitality and Mediterranean cuisine, Emilie excels at matching Provence rosé with food—from salade Niçoise and bouillabaisse to cheese boards and beyond.

Provence wine producers

Emilie works with renowned producers including Domaines Ott, Château Minuty, Château Miraval, and Château d'Esclans, selecting wines that express the true character of Provençal terroir.
